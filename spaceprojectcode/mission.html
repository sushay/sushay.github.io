Mission Hebe

Mission Hebe gets its designation from the Greek Goddess of youth. The inagural long term (+90 day) mission of Juventas, our mission will travel around Mars, depositing a satellite into it's uppper atmosphere before returning to Earth. It will serves as an educational stress test for our first cohert as well as a data collection mission for atmoshperic weather data. The Hebe astornauts will observe one seasonal period for Mars, with continuing data provided by the satellite will be anazlyed on the return trip to Earth.

A joint endvor between the Juvenatas educatioal and training program and the Space X rocket development team, Hebe serves as a landmark event in the collection of astrophysical data.
